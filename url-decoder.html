Frequently Asked Questions

What is URL decoding?

URL decoding converts percent-encoded characters (like %20) back to their original form (like a space).

How do I use this tool?

Paste your encoded URL or text, click "Decode", and the decoded result will appear below. Click "Copy" to copy it to your clipboard.

Why do I need to decode URLs?

Decoding makes URLs and data readable again after being transmitted in a safe, encoded format.

Can I copy the decoded result?

Yes! Click the "Copy" button next to the decoded result to copy it to your clipboard.

What if my input is not encoded?

If your input is not encoded, the tool will simply return the original text.
